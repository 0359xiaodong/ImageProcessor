Crop

Crops the current image to the given location and size.

Crop(Rectangle rectangle)

Parameters

rectangle
The System.Drawing.Rectangle containing the coordinates to crop the image to.
Crop(CropLayer cropLayer)

Parameters

CropLayer
The ImageProcessor.Imaging.CropLayer containing the following properties required to crop the image.
Left
The System.Float defining the left coordinate of the crop layer.
Top
The System.Float defining the top coordinate of the crop layer.
Right
The System.Float defining the right coordinate of the crop layer.
Bottom
The System.Float defining the bottom coordinate of the crop layer.
