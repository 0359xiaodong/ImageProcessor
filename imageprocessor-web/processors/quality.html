Quality

Alters the output quality of the current image. This method will only effect the output quality of jpeg images.

Whilst ImageProcessor.Web delivers an excellent quality/filesize ratio it also allows you to change the quality of jpegs on-the-fly.

Requests are passed thus: 

http://your-image?quality=65
